Hungry? well take a look at our local delicacies

Well pakistan has alot of tasty food, so i decided to break it down into 4 catergories breakfast, lunch, dinner , desserts

BreakfastLunchDinnerDessert

Breakfast: Halwa Puri is a comforting, hearty meal that's popular in India and Pakistan for weekend breakfast or during the festival season. It's made of puri (or fried bread) and a sweet halwa with a semolina base for a delicious balance of sweet and savory flavors. It's often accompanied by a yellow or black chickpeas curry. - Masala And Chai

Lunch: Chicken Karahi is a spicy dish that is prepared in a karahi (type of wok). It is usually eaten with naan, roti, or rice. It is a very famous dish in Pakistan and India.

Dinner: Despite fan-favorite dish Biryani, I would like to introduce you to Nihari. This is a stew dish usually made of beef, lamb, and mutton, usually topped with ginger cut in small long strips. It is a slow-cooked dish and enjoyed with Naan

Dessert: Kheer is a pudding usually made on days of celebration, such as Eid in Pakistan. It is usually made with rice or seviyaan (vermicelli).

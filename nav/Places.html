My Favourite Places

Badshahi Mosque is located in Lahore, when it was built in the 16th century, it was the largest mosque in the world, now it is the 2nd largest mosque in Pakistan and 15th Largest in the world. The Mosque was Built in 2 years which reelected the Great strength and Wealth of The Mughal Empire.

The Image Shows Faisal Mosque located in Islamabad. It Is the 5th most biggest Mosque worldwide, the design is made to resemble a Bedouins tent. The construction began in 1976 when King Faisal of Saudi Arabia granted a $28 million grant for the construction of the mosque. It has capacity to hold over 300,000 worshippers.

Khunjerab Pass is a high mountain pass in the Karakoram Highway, which is located at an elevation of 4,693 meters (15,397 feet) above sea level. The karakoram Highway links Pakistan and China and it passes through Khunjerab Pass, the pass is closed in winter due to heavy snow but opens during summer and acts as a tourism attraction and place for trade.

K2 is the 2nd highest Mountain worldwide , taking its place after Mount Everest. It is Located in the Karakoram Range with a Whopping elevation of 8,611 meters it is estimated that one in four or five summiteers die on this mountain, which is 22% of all climbers die, on mount Everest this rate is estimated to be 3%.

Rohtas Fort is situated near City of Jhelum, Built by Afghan King Sher Shah Suri in the 16th century, it was strategic fort placed to guard against the Mughals. It was Named UNESCO World Heritage Site in 1997. The main fortifications are the massive walls that are over 4km long.

Kalash Valley is a remote tribe in Pakistan with vibrant culture located in the region of Khyber Pakhtunkhwa.
